Frequently Asked Questions

How do document limits work?
Document limits refer to the number of unique documents processed per month. If you exceed your plan's limit, you can upgrade or purchase additional capacity.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan. Contact our sales team for Enterprise or Custom plan trials.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for annual subscriptions. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we are SOC 2 Type II certified and use enterprise-grade encryption. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we never train models on customer data.
Do you offer annual discounts?
Yes, annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount. Contact our sales team to learn more about volume discounts for enterprise agreements.
